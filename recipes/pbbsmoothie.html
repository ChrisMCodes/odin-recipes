Peanut Butter and Banana Smoothie

Description

These delicious peanut butter and banana smoothies are sweet, creamy, and delicious. Each serving contains more than 12g protein to help you feel satisfied and ready to take on the day. These are a great option for people on the go or simply needing a little pick-me-up to get ready for a morning workout.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • ½ cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, approximately 45 seconds.
  3. Enjoy!
